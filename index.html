Project Idea

This part shows my web based project idea, to be more specific, this is Advanced Transportation Training Driving School Management System, Directions and Update Traffic Situation which is called Supporting Transportation Apps. It can support for people who are driving and want to know about the unpleasant driving experiences such as weather changes, accidents, or congestion. Analyzing the car speed, suggesting the suitable speed to avoid exceeding the allowed speed, the shortest and the fastest way to destination. Moreover, people can sign up for training driving school and booking vehicles or instructors for training. This can prevent the efforts of human power and wealth, additionally, make sure that driving-training information can be utilized effectively.
